# Product Specifications

## Discount Rules
- The discount code **SAVE15** applies a **15% discount** to the total price.
- Only one discount code can be applied per order.
- Invalid or expired codes must show the error: "Invalid discount code".

## Shipping Rules
- Standard shipping is **free**.
- Express shipping adds **$10** to the total price.

## Product Prices
- Item A: $20
- Item B: $35
- Item C: $10
